# 🛡️ OptiMix

> HUD & utility mod for Minecraft that gives you control of your screen.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🛡️ Armor HUD
Displays your equipped armor pieces directly on screen.  
Configurable side, rotation, and hotbar locking.

---

### 🔧 Item Durability Bar
A dedicated durability indicator for the item in your hand.  
Fully repositionable with X/Y offset controls.

---

### ⚗️ Effects HUD
Replaces the default potion effect overlay with a compact, readable list.  
Configurable left or right side positioning.

---

### ⚔️ Damage Bar
Visual indicator for incoming and outgoing damage.  
Real-time combat feedback with no extra mods required.

---

### ☀️ Full Bright
Toggleable maximum brightness.  
No gamma edits or resource packs — see everything in dark areas instantly.

---

### 🎃 Pumpkin Overlay Settings
Control the pumpkin helmet overlay — default, fully transparent, or subtle tint.  
No more blocked vision.

---

### 🔍 Item Inspector
Hover over any item to see a full breakdown of NBT data, components, enchantments, and custom attributes.

---

### 📦 Recipe Creator *(Create compat)*
In-game crafting recipe builder with native Create mod support.  
Design and preview recipes without leaving the game.
